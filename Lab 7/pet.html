Pet Animals

Dogs

Dogs are loyal companions known for their unconditional love and friendship. They come in various breeds, each with unique characteristics and temperaments. Dogs are social animals that form strong bonds with their human families.

Cats

Cats are independent and graceful pets that make wonderful companions. They are known for their agility, hunting instincts, and affectionate nature when bonded with their owners. Cats are also excellent at keeping themselves clean.

Rabbits

Rabbits are gentle and social creatures that make great indoor pets. They are intelligent, can be litter-trained, and show affection to their caregivers. Rabbits require regular exercise and enjoy playing with toys.
